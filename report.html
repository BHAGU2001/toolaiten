7.Define Calculations and Metrics:Define calculated fields, metrics, and key performance indicators (KPIs) based on your analysis requirements.
8.Schedule and Automate Reports:Schedule reports to run at specific times or events. Automation ensures that the reports are generated and distributed according to your predefined schedule.
9.Collaborate and Share Insights:Collaborate with team members by sharing reports and insights. SAS BI tools often provide options for sharing reports via email, dashboards, or collaboration platforms.
10.Monitor and Optimize:Monitor the performance of your reports and dashboards. Optimize queries and data sources for better efficiency.
11.Security and Access Control:Implement security measures to control access to sensitive data. Define roles and permissions to ensure that users have the appropriate level of access.
12.Training and Documentation:Provide training to users on how to use SAS BI tools effectively. Create documentation for reference.
